## 2. Calculator Problems

### 2.1 Input and Calculation Issues

#### Problem: Calculator Not Responding
**Symptoms**: Clicking calculate button does nothing
**Causes**: 
- JavaScript errors
- Browser compatibility issues
- Network connectivity problems
- Browser extension interference

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. **Disable browser extensions** temporarily
3. **Clear browser cache** and cookies
4. **Try incognito/private mode**
5. **Update browser** to latest version
6. **Try different browser**

#### Problem: "Invalid Input" Error
**Symptoms**: Calculator shows "Invalid input" for valid values
**Common Causes**:
- Non-numeric characters in numeric fields
- Missing required fields
- Values outside acceptable ranges
- Special characters in input

**Solutions**:
1. **Check all input fields** for correct format
2. **Use only numbers** (0-9) and decimal points
3. **Ensure required fields** are filled
4. **Verify values are within ranges**:
   - Volume: 250ml - 10,000ml
   - Caffeine: 30-150mg per serving
   - Servings: 1-50 servings
5. **Clear and re-enter values**

#### Problem: Calculation Results Seem Wrong
**Symptoms**: Results don't match expected values or seem inaccurate
**Causes**:
- Input value errors
- Unit conversion mistakes
- Rounding differences
- Calculator logic errors

**Solutions**:
1. **Double-check input values** for accuracy
2. **Verify unit selections** (ml vs liters, mg vs grams)
3. **Recalculate manually** to verify results
4. **Try different input combinations** to test consistency
5. **Report systematic errors** to support team

#### Problem: Calculator Freezes During Calculation
**Symptoms**: Page becomes unresponsive during calculation
**Solutions**:
1. **Wait 30 seconds** for processing to complete
2. **Check browser console** for JavaScript errors
3. **Close other browser tabs** to free memory
4. **Restart browser** and try again
5. **Try on different device** to isolate issue

### 2.2 Display and Interface Issues

#### Problem: Results Not Displaying
**Symptoms**: Calculation completes but no results shown
**Solutions**:
1. **Scroll down** - results may be below current view
2. **Refresh page** and recalculate
3. **Check if JavaScript is enabled**
4. **Clear browser cache**
5. **Try different browser**

#### Problem: Safety Indicators Missing

2. **Check browser zoom level** (should be 100%)
3. **Clear browser cache** and reload

#### Problem: Export/Print Not Working

4. **Use browser's print function** (Ctrl+P)

## 7. Frequently Asked Questions

### 7.1 General Questions

#### Q: Is this application free to use?
**A**: Yes, the application is completely free. We may include affiliate links to suppliers, but there's no cost to use the calculator or access any features.

#### Q: Do I need to create an account?
**A**: No account is required. All data is stored locally in your browser. This means your recipes and preferences stay private on your device.

#### Q: Is my data safe and private?
**A**: Yes. We follow GDPR guidelines and don't store personal data. All calculations are done locally in your browser. We don't track or store your recipes unless you choose to share them.

#### Q: Can I use this offline?
**A**: Yes, with limited functionality. Core features work offline after the initial online load. Your saved recipes and basic calculator functions are available offline.

#### Q: Is this available in other languages?
**A**: Currently available in English and Dutch. We may add more languages based on user demand.

### 7.2 Safety Questions

#### Q: Is it safe to make energy drinks at home?
**A**: When following our safety guidelines and EU regulations, home energy drink creation can be safe. However, you must use proper equipment (especially a 0.001g precision scale for caffeine) and follow safety procedures exactly.

#### Q: What if I exceed the safety limits?
**A**: The calculator will warn you if you exceed EU safety limits. NEVER ignore these warnings. Reduce caffeine concentration or volume to safe levels before proceeding.

#### Q: Can I give energy drinks to minors?
**A**: No. Our application requires 18+ age verification and we strongly advise against giving energy drinks to minors due to health and safety concerns.

#### Q: Do I need medical consultation?
**A**: Recommended if you have any health conditions, take medications, or are unsure about caffeine consumption. Consult healthcare providers for personalized advice.

### 7.3 Technical Questions

#### Q: Why do I need a 0.001g scale?
**A**: Caffeine is lethal in small amounts. Kitchen scales aren't precise enough for safe measurements. A 0.001g precision scale is mandatory for safe caffeine handling.

#### Q: Can I use different suppliers?
**A**: Yes, but verify ingredient quality and purity. We recommend EU-approved suppliers listed in our guides for safety and quality assurance.

#### Q: How accurate are the calculations?
**A**: Calculations are precise when proper equipment is used. Always double-check measurements and follow preparation sequences exactly as calculated.

#### Q: Can I modify the recipes?
**A**: Yes, the recipe system allows for customization. Start with proven recipes and make small, documented changes. Always maintain safety parameters.

### 7.4 Usage Questions

#### Q: How do I scale recipes for large batches?
**A**: Use the calculator's scaling features or manually scale ingredients proportionally. Test scaling with small batches first to verify results.

#### Q: Can I share my recipes?
**A**: Yes, you can export recipes and share them. Recipe sharing features are built into the application for easy distribution.

#### Q: How do I organize many recipes?
**A**: Use categories, tags, and the search function. The recipe management system supports organization by flavor type, base, caffeine level, and custom tags.

#### Q: What's the best base type for beginners?
**A**: Classic base is recommended for beginners as it provides familiar taste and better mouthfeel. Zero base is good for sugar-free options.

## 9. Emergency Procedures

### 9.1 Caffeine Overdose Response

#### Immediate Symptoms to Watch For
- **Rapid or irregular heartbeat**
- **Severe anxiety or panic**
- **Nausea and vomiting**
- **Dizziness or confusion**
- **Trembling or shaking**
- **Difficulty breathing**

#### Emergency Actions
1. **STOP consuming** the energy drink immediately
2. **Call emergency services** (112 in Netherlands)
3. **Provide information**:
   - Amount of caffeine consumed
   - Time of consumption
   - Current symptoms
   - Age and weight if known

#### Information for Medical Personnel
- **Exact amount** of caffeine consumed
- **Other ingredients** in the drink
- **Time of consumption**
- **Current symptoms**
- **Any medications** being taken
- **Medical history** (heart conditions, etc.)

### 9.2 Chemical Exposure Response

#### Skin Contact with Caffeine
1. **Remove contaminated clothing**
2. **Flush with water** for at least 15 minutes
3. **Seek medical attention** if irritation occurs
4. **Bring product information** to medical facility

#### Eye Contact with Chemicals
1. **Flush immediately** with clean water for 15 minutes
2. **Remove contact lenses** if easily removable
3. **Seek immediate medical attention**
4. **Do not rub eyes**

### 9.3 Technical Emergency

#### Application Security Concerns
1. **Stop using** the application immediately
2. **Disconnect from internet** if security threat suspected
3. **Contact support** via emergency channels
4. **Document any** suspicious activity
