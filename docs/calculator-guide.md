## üî¢ Input Parameters Explained

### Base Type Selection

#### Classic Base
**Best for**: Traditional energy drink taste and texture
- **Sugar Content**: Includes regular sugar for taste and mouthfeel
- **Calories**: Higher calorie content
- **Shelf Life**: Natural preservation from sugar
- **Taste**: Full-bodied, familiar energy drink flavor

**Ingredients included**:
- Caffeine anhydrous
- Taurine
- Citric acid
- Sodium citrate
- Sugar (sucrose)
- Preservatives (sodium benzoate, potassium sorbate)

#### Zero Base
**Best for**: Low-calorie, sugar-free energy drinks
- **Sugar Content**: Sugar-free with artificial sweeteners
- **Calories**: Very low calorie content
- **Shelf Life**: Requires preservatives for stability
- **Taste**: Crisp, clean taste with sweetener aftertaste

**Ingredients included**:
- Caffeine anhydrous
- Taurine
- Citric acid
- Sodium citrate
- Erythritol (natural sweetener)
- Sucralose (optional, for enhanced sweetness)
- Preservatives (sodium benzoate, potassium sorbate)

#### Plain Base
**Best for**: Minimal ingredients, maximum customization
- **Sugar Content**: No sugar or sweeteners included
- **Calories**: Zero calories from base
- **Shelf Life**: Requires immediate consumption or refrigeration
- **Taste**: Neutral base for custom flavoring

**Ingredients included**:
- Caffeine anhydrous
- Taurine
- Citric acid
- Sodium citrate
- Minimal preservatives

### Ingredient Measurements

#### Precise Quantities
The calculator provides exact measurements for:
- **Caffeine**: Measured in grams (0.001g precision required)
- **Taurine**: Measured in grams
- **Acids**: Citric acid and sodium citrate in grams
- **Sweeteners**: Sugar or erythritol in grams
- **Preservatives**: Sodium benzoate and potassium sorbate in grams

#### Measurement Units
- **Solids**: Grams (g)
- **Liquids**: Milliliters (ml) or grams (g)
- **Concentrates**: Milliliters (ml)
- **Powders**: Grams (g) with 0.001g precision

#### Equipment Requirements
- **0.001g Precision Scale**: Mandatory for caffeine measurement
- **Graduated Cylinders**: For liquid measurements
- **Digital Pipettes**: For precise concentrate measurement
- **Digital Thermometer**: For temperature monitoring

### Safety Status Indicators

#### Color-Coded Safety Levels

**üü¢ GREEN - Safe**
- All parameters within EU guidelines
- No safety warnings
- Proceed with confidence

**üü° YELLOW - Caution**
- Parameters approach safety limits
- Additional precautions recommended
- Consider reducing caffeine or volume

**üî¥ RED - Unsafe**
- Parameters exceed safety limits
- DO NOT proceed with calculation
- Modify inputs to safe levels

#### Safety Validation Checks

1. **Caffeine per Serving**
   - Checked against 80mg EU limit

   - Block if exceeds 120mg

2. **Daily Caffeine Intake**
   - Calculated from serving size and frequency
   - Checked against 400mg daily limit
   - Warning if daily total exceeds 400mg

3. **Concentration Safety**
   - Caffeine per 100ml check
   - Maximum 150mg per 100ml
   - Additional warnings for high concentrations
