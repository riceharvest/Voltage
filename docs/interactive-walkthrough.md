### Step 4: Basic Calculation

**Objective**: Perform your first energy drink calculation.

#### Actions to Perform:
1. **Select "Classic" base type**
2. **Enter target volume**: 500 (ml)
3. **Enter caffeine concentration**: 80 (mg per serving)
4. **Click "Calculate" or let auto-calculate**
5. **Review the results**

#### Input Values:
```

Volume: 500 ml
Caffeine: 80 mg/serving
Servings: 1
```

#### What You'll See:
- Ingredient list with precise measurements

- Safety validation results
- Warning indicators if limits exceeded

#### Success Criteria:
